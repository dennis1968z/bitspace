How it works

Think → Do → Check → Improve auto work loop

A work loop every 5–8 minutes — when new capabilities are needed, it triggers FTaaS fine-tuning automatically.

Think · plan & design→ Do · execute & call tools→ Check · internal review & CEO score→ Improve · distill & refine SOPs↻

The four Harness principles

1

Chat UI · boss mode

You give direction and taste; the platform executes. Talk to a team, not a piece of software.

2

Shared memory + self-improvement

Remembers your taste, worldview and SOPs long-term, refining itself every loop.

3

Sandbox + rollback

Experiment in parallel, fail fast, auto-rollback — business continuity and safe boundaries.

4

Webhook + tool integration

20+ external tools across 5 categories, fully automated: social, comms, office, payments, DevOps.
