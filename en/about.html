About BitSpace

Let everyone
run a whole team

Our vision: that every developer and creator can run their own One-Person Company 24/7 through a cloud Agent Matrix, serving small-business customers worldwide. BitSpace stands across Singapore and Silicon Valley, connecting APAC compute with global demand.

Our mission

Put enterprise-grade productivity
in everyone's pocket

In 2026, the "one-person company" is becoming a global trend. As AI agents and infrastructure mature, "one person running a whole team" turns from slogan into reality. BitSpace holds both ends — at the bottom we are an OCI partner for Asia-Pacific delivering cost-efficient, compliant infrastructure; at the top, OPC OS turns that compute into a business that runs itself 24/7.

  • Supply side: developers, creators and independent founders
  • Demand side: overseas SMBs, creators and local service providers
  • Foundation: Oracle Cloud Infrastructure + hybrid open-model strategy
