Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files a website stores on your device to make it work, remember your preferences, and help us understand how the site is used.

2. Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies (always on)

Enable core functionality such as security, load balancing and recording your cookie consent choice. These cannot be turned off. Example: bs_cookie_consent (records your consent state, stored locally in your browser).

Analytics cookies (require consent)

Measure traffic, sources and page performance to help us improve. Enabled only after you click "Accept".

Marketing cookies (require consent)

Measure the effectiveness of marketing. Not enabled by default on this site; if enabled in future, we will update this page and seek your consent.

3. Managing your consent

You can choose "Accept" or "Essential only" via the cookie banner on first visit. To change your choice, clear this site's local storage and cookies in your browser and revisit — the banner will reappear. You can also block or delete cookies via your browser settings.

4. Third-party cookies

When we enable analytics or embed third-party services (e.g. video), those third parties may set their own cookies, governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. More information

For how we handle personal data, see our Privacy Policy. Questions: Dennis@bitspace.sg.
