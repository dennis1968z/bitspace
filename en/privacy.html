1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data collected and processed by BitSpace Pte. Ltd. ("BitSpace", "we"; Singapore UEN: [TBD]) through this website, the OPC OS platform and our cloud services. We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

2. Data we collect

  • Information you provide: name, email, phone/WeChat, company or project, and inquiry content (via contact/waitlist forms).
  • Account & usage data: registration details, sign-in records, in-platform project and agent configuration (registered users only).
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device info, timestamps and pages (via logs and cookies).
  • Customer business data: content processed by OPC operators in the platform, owned by that operator; BitSpace acts only as a data processor.

3. How we use it

  • Respond to inquiries; provide demos and sales support;
  • Provide, maintain and improve the website and platform;
  • Account management, billing and subscriptions;
  • Security, abuse detection and compliance audits;
  • Product updates and marketing, where you have consented.

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

We process data on one or more of: performance of a contract, your consent, our legitimate interests (e.g. security and improvement), and compliance with legal obligations.

5. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share only as necessary with the following categories of providers, under equivalent protection standards:

  • Cloud & infrastructure: Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI);
  • Forms & communication: form processing and email providers;
  • Payments: Stripe, PayPal, etc. (where applicable);
  • Analytics: website analytics enabled after your consent.

6. Cross-border transfers

BitSpace operates nodes in Singapore (primary) and Qingdao, China (compliance/isolated). Data of users in China is stored in the Qingdao node under data-localization requirements; overseas data is stored in Singapore under GDPR. Where transfers occur, we use lawful mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

7. Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, or longer where required by law. Inquiry data is typically kept up to 24 months after resolution; account data for a reasonable period after account closure.

8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may access, correct or delete your personal data, restrict or object to processing, request portability, and withdraw consent. To exercise your rights, contact the email below; we will respond within statutory timeframes.

9. Security

We protect data with encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, a permission sandbox and a Credential Vault. We are pursuing ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certification (in progress).
